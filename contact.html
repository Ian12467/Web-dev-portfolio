Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some common questions about my services.

How long does it take to complete a website?

The timeline for a website project depends on its complexity. A simple website can be completed in 2-3 weeks, while more complex projects may take 1-2 months. I'll provide you with a specific timeline after discussing your requirements.

What is your pricing structure?

I offer different pricing packages based on the scope of the project. Basic websites start at $499, while more complex websites with e-commerce functionality or custom features may cost more. I provide detailed quotes after understanding your specific needs.

Do you provide website maintenance services?

Yes, I offer website maintenance services to ensure your website remains up-to-date, secure, and functioning properly. This includes regular updates, security patches, backups, and technical support.

Can you help with website hosting?

Yes, I can help you set up hosting for your website. I work with reliable hosting providers to ensure your website has high uptime, fast loading speeds, and regular backups.
